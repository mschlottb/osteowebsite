Welcome to the OSTEO Project

This project explores the complex and fascinating process of osseointegration through a combination of scientific research and creative communication. Our goal is to evaluate the methods used to assess the integration between bone and implant surfaces, analyze the impact of surface design and materials, and present our findings in the form of a podcast and web experience.
